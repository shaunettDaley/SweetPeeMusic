Get To Know Sweet Pee


Singer song writer Shanna Marie Carty A.K.A " SWEET PEE " born October 7 to the parents of Myrtle and Kingsley Carty second eldest of 6 children in Jamaica known for reggae legendary musician artist such as Mr. Bob Marley , Peter Tosh , Gregory Issac and many others.

Music was always apart of her life , Sweet Pee's interest in music development started at a very young age.While she started singing around the house with her sibblings , music was always apart of her craft . Great performers like Aretha Franklin , Diana Ross , Gladys Knight ( to name a few ) were all phenomenal ladies whome paved the way for some of the hits we hear today . It gave her a deep connection to what will become the high light of her life .

While attending Barracks Roads Primary School , Sweet Pee took part in many activites while there such as , the school choir , drama , dance club . She also met Robbie Dunbar and other influential people along the way . As recognition for her choreography talent grew solid along with her passion for singing and songwriting. Then in 2015 she decided to put her pen to action and wrote out her first record " OVER YOU " at a friends studio the song went wild getting over 10,000 views in less then a week on WZPP radio 96.1.

Although Sweet Pee's story is still yet unwritten , the best is yet to come ..... SO STAY TUNE WORLD
